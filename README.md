# Kaggle Gen AI Intensive Course

A comprehensive 5-day hands-on program focused on mastering the core concepts and applications of Generative AI — from foundational models to production-level deployment.

---

## What's Covered

### Day 1: Foundational Models & Prompt Engineering
- Explore the evolution of Large Language Models (LLMs) — from transformers to fine-tuning, inference acceleration, and reasoning models.  
- Master the art of prompt engineering to interact effectively with LLMs and achieve optimal results.

---

### Day 2: Embeddings and Vector Stores/Databases
- Understand the concepts behind embeddings and vector databases.  
- Learn about embedding methods, vector search algorithms, and real-world LLM applications, along with their trade-offs.

---

### Day 3: Generative AI Agents
- Learn to build intelligent AI agents from scratch.  
- Explore the core components, iterative development process, and the latest agent frameworks like Agentspace.

---

### Day 4: Domain-Specific LLMs
- Dive into specialized language models such as SecLM and Med-PaLM.  
- Gain insights from the researchers behind these models and understand their real-world use cases.

---

### Day 5: MLOps for Generative AI
- Learn how to apply MLOps best practices to Generative AI.  
- Explore Vertex AI tools for managing foundation models and deploying scalable generative AI applications.

---

## Summary
This course offers an end-to-end journey through the world of Generative AI — perfect for data scientists, ML engineers, and AI enthusiasts looking to deepen their technical understanding and practical skills.
